About

The Central Ohio Railroad Prototype Models (RPM) meet is held annually in Marion Ohio at the old Marion Union Station. Visit the home page for more information.

Subscribe to the Central Ohio RPM Facebook page to interact with others interested in the event. The event is organized by Denis Blake, join the facebook page. Email Denis directly using the email in the image below

To contact Denis Blake directly use email: seaboard_1966+corpm2025@yahoo.com

The Marion Unition Station Association takes care of the depot and coordinates other events too, including Summer Rail.
